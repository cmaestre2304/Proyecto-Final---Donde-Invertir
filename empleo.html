Nuestras Ofertas de Empleo

Technical Support Engineer

The Technical Support Engineer provides with remote first-tier and second-tier Information Technology (IT) support to end-users, to restore service and/or identify and the correct core problem. Working knowledge of infrastructure and Cisco networks (switch, routers, etc.). Working knowledge of Microsoft server, workstation and networking technologies.

Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to join our ever-growing team. In this role, you will detect trends and generate reports to propose new editorial content. Additionally, our ideal candidate is a fast and flexible go-getter who jumps on new opportunities to create impactful work. Experience with data visualization tools such as Google Suite, Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, YouTube Analytics, Data Studio, Klipfolio or some other.

Front-End Developer SSr.

The front end team will be in charge of developing all the improvements of the mobile and web applications. Build complex screens and UI libraries. Integrate modern API's into web and native apps. Work collaboratively with product designers. Actively participate in product development. Experience working with GraphQL, Next JS, Storybook y React Native Web. Experience working with maps and navigation apps.

Back-End Developer Jr.

As a backend engineer at Donde Invertir, you will have to build and maintain new features for our main product. Strong experience with Python Django Rest Framework. Knows deeply when to prefer SQL over NoSQL and when Queues are the answer. Prior experience employing CI/CD principles (proper testing, clean code). Daily experience working with development tools like git and Linux systems.

UX/UI Designer

You are part of the process where we generate ideas, create prototypes, and visually design them before these concepts will be developed. we ask from you: to have a background in Digital Product Design, to have experience in environments where User Testing and UX Research is part of the process, to have working knowledge of CSS & experience working closely with front end development.
